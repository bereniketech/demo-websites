# 1. Clinic Demo — “Appointment + Trust Machine”

### Concept:

A modern clinic site that feels like a hospital-level system, but simple enough for a small practice.

## Structure:

### Hero

* Doctor + clinic image
* Headline: “Trusted Care for Your Family”
* CTA: “Book Appointment”

---

### Doctor Profile

* Name, qualifications, experience
* Specialization
* Short credibility statement

---

### Services

* General Consultation
* Skin Treatment
* Dental Care / Physio (depending on niche)
* Each with short description

---

### Appointment Booking

* Name
* Phone
* Problem selection
* Preferred time

👉 “Book Consultation” button

---

### Testimonials

* Patient reviews
* “Quick diagnosis, very professional”

---

### Location + Contact

* Map
* WhatsApp button
* Call button

---

👉 **Goal:** turn visits into booked appointments
